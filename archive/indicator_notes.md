# Indicator Notes

## Indicator Definitions
This document contains detailed notes about key performance indicators (KPIs) and metrics.

## NICU/SNCU Indicators
- **Admission Rate**: Percentage of admitted patients
- **Discharge Rate**: Successful discharge percentage
- **Mortality Rate**: Patient mortality metrics
- **Length of Stay**: Average duration of care
- **Readmission Rate**: Patients readmitted within 7 days

## Mother Care Indicators
- **Antenatal Coverage**: Prenatal care coverage
- **Delivery Mode**: Vaginal vs Cesarean deliveries
- **Postpartum Complications**: Complication tracking
- **Breastfeeding Initiation**: Nursing success rate

## Quality Indicators
- **Data Completeness**: Percentage of complete records
- **Data Accuracy**: Validation metrics
- **Timeliness**: Data entry timeliness
- **Consistency**: Cross-dataset consistency
